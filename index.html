Spirited Away

Spirited Away tells the story of Chihiro, a young girl who finds herself trapped in a mysterious and magical world of spirits. To return to the human world, she must work in a bathhouse for the spirits and find her inner strength.

Main Cast
  • Chihiro: Rumi Hiiragi
  • Haku: Miyu Irino
  • Yubaba: Mari Natsuki
  • Kamaji: Bunta Sugawara
Trivia

It is the only non-English language film to win the Academy Award for Best Animated Feature. The movie was made without a script; Hayao Miyazaki wrote the story as he drew the storyboard. Spirited Away overtook Titanic's box office record in Japan when released.
